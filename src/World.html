Instructions

Help Waldo! Build a bridge from directly below Waldo to the green cube. The bridge must be flat and be directly below Waldo and the green cube. Press the "Check Waldo" button to check if Waldo can escape. NOTE: Firefox is not supported. Please use Chrome.

To place blocks, right click. To destroy, left click. To disable the highlighted block from appearing, press middle click. Refresh to change the positions.
